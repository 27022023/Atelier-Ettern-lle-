Aesthetic Library

Explore popular aesthetics and learn how to recreate each look through makeup, hair, outfits, and vibes.

Clean Girl

  • Makeup: Dewy skin, fluffy brows, glossy lips.
  • Hair: Sleek bun or soft waves.
  • Outfits: Neutrals, basics, gold jewelry.

Coquette

  • Makeup: Blushy cheeks, soft pink lips.
  • Hair: Ribbons, curls, soft bangs.
  • Outfits: Lace, bows, pastel colors.

Old Money

  • Makeup: Minimal, polished.
  • Hair: Blowout or sleek ponytail.
  • Outfits: Blazers, trousers, neutral tones.

Dark Feminine

  • Makeup: Smokey eyes, bold lips.
  • Hair: Sleek, dramatic styles.
  • Outfits: Black, deep reds, structured silhouettes.

Soft Glam

  • Makeup: Warm tones, lashes, glowing skin.
  • Hair: Hollywood waves or soft curls.
  • Outfits: Satin, neutrals, gold accents.
