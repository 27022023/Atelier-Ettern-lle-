Aesthetic Library

Explore popular aesthetics and learn how to recreate each look through makeup, hair, outfits, colors, and overall vibe.

Aesthetics are more than outfits — they’re a whole vibe. This guide breaks down makeup, hair, fashion, colors, accessories, and energy for each aesthetic so you can build your own signature style.

Clean Girl

  • Makeup: Dewy skin, fluffy brows, soft blush, glossy lips.
  • Hair: Sleek bun, claw clip, soft waves.
  • Outfits: Neutrals, basics, matching sets, gold jewelry.
  • Colors: White, beige, sage, soft brown.
  • Accessories: Gold hoops, tote bags, minimal sneakers.
  • Vibe: Effortless, fresh, organized.

Coquette

  • Makeup: Blushy cheeks, soft pink lips, fluttery lashes.
  • Hair: Ribbons, curls, soft bangs.
  • Outfits: Lace, bows, pastel skirts, dainty tops.
  • Colors: Baby pink, cream, lavender.
  • Accessories: Pearls, ribbons, heart motifs.
  • Vibe: Romantic, dreamy, feminine.

Old Money

  • Makeup: Minimal, polished, soft matte.
  • Hair: Blowout, sleek ponytail, soft curls.
  • Outfits: Blazers, trousers, cashmere, tailored pieces.
  • Colors: Navy, cream, camel, black.
  • Accessories: Silk scarves, structured bags, loafers.
  • Vibe: Elegant, timeless, refined.

Dark Feminine

  • Makeup: Smokey eyes, bold lips, sharp liner.
  • Hair: Sleek straight hair, dramatic waves.
  • Outfits: Black, deep reds, leather, structured silhouettes.
  • Colors: Burgundy, black, wine, silver.
  • Accessories: Statement jewelry, heels, dark nails.
  • Vibe: Powerful, mysterious, confident.

Soft Glam

  • Makeup: Warm tones, lashes, glowing skin.
  • Hair: Hollywood waves, soft curls, blowouts.
  • Outfits: Satin, neutrals, gold accents.
  • Colors: Champagne, nude, rose gold.
  • Accessories: Gold necklaces, soft fabrics, heels.
  • Vibe: Feminine, polished, radiant.

Boho

  • Makeup: Natural tones, bronzed skin.
  • Hair: Loose waves, braids, messy buns.
  • Outfits: Flowy dresses, crochet tops, earthy prints.
  • Colors: Terracotta, cream, olive, mustard.
  • Accessories: Layered necklaces, beads, fringe bags.
  • Vibe: Free‑spirited, earthy, artistic.

Y2K

  • Makeup: Glossy lips, frosty eyeshadow, blushy cheeks.
  • Hair: Butterfly clips, high pony, flipped ends.
  • Outfits: Mini skirts, baby tees, low‑rise jeans.
  • Colors: Hot pink, baby blue, silver.
  • Accessories: Chunky rings, tiny bags, sparkles.
  • Vibe: Fun, bold, nostalgic.

Dark & Light Academia

  • Makeup: Soft matte, neutral tones.
  • Hair: Loose curls, messy buns, natural texture.
  • Outfits: Sweaters, plaid skirts, trousers.
  • Colors: Dark: Brown, black, forest green. Light: Cream, beige, soft brown.
  • Accessories: Books, glasses, satchels.
  • Vibe: Intellectual, cozy, vintage.

Baddie

  • Makeup: Full glam, contour, lashes.
  • Hair: Sleek middle part, long layers, high pony.
  • Outfits: Bodycon, cargos, crop tops.
  • Colors: Black, nude, red.
  • Accessories: Statement jewelry, designer‑inspired pieces.
  • Vibe: Confident, bold, glamorous.

Soft Girl

  • Makeup: Pink blush, glossy lips, sparkly eyes.
  • Hair: Fluffy curls, braids, ribbons.
  • Outfits: Pastels, skirts, cozy sweaters.
  • Colors: Pink, lavender, cream.
  • Accessories: Hearts, bows, pearls.
  • Vibe: Cute, gentle, dreamy.
