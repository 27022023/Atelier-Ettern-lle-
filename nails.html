Nail Inspiration

Metallic turquoise vibes, soft silver glow, and endless nail ideas to match your aesthetic.

Whether you want soft girl nails, bold statement claws, or clean minimalist vibes, this guide gives you shapes, colors, designs, seasonal ideas, care routines, and a quiz to find your perfect nail aesthetic.

Nail Shapes

Each shape gives a different energy. Choose based on your vibe, lifestyle, and nail length.

  • Almond: Elegant, soft, universally flattering.
  • Coffin/Ballerina: Trendy, bold, perfect for long nails.
  • Square: Clean, classic, great for short nails.
  • Oval: Natural, feminine, elongates fingers.
  • Stiletto: Dramatic, edgy, high‑fashion.

Nail & Hand Care Routine

Healthy nails = beautiful nails. Here’s a simple routine:

  • Daily: Apply hand cream + cuticle oil.
  • Weekly: File nails gently in one direction.
  • Every 2 weeks: Push back cuticles (don’t cut them).
  • Monthly: Give nails a break from polish.
  • Always: Wear gloves when cleaning.

Cuticle oil ideas: jojoba oil, almond oil, vitamin E oil.
