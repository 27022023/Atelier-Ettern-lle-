Beauty Guide

Makeup Placement Guide

Blush Placement by Face Shape

  • Round: Higher on the cheekbones, angled slightly up toward the temples to lift.
  • Oval: Center of the cheeks, blended outward for a soft, natural flush.
  • Square: Focus on the apples and blend in circular motions to soften angles.
  • Heart: Slightly lower on the cheeks to balance a wider forehead.
  • Diamond: On the tops of the cheeks, blended outward to soften sharpness.
  • Long: More horizontal placement across the cheeks to visually shorten the face.

Highlighter Placement

  • Top of cheekbones, bridge of nose (lightly), cupid’s bow, inner corners of eyes.
  • Avoid heavy highlight on textured areas to keep skin looking smooth.

Contour Basics

  • Under cheekbones, sides of nose, jawline, and temples (lightly).
  • Cream contour = more natural; powder contour = more defined.

Eyeshadow & Eye Shape

General Tips

  • Light shades on the lid, medium in the crease, dark on the outer corner.
  • Shimmer on the center of the lid or inner corner for brightness.

By Eye Shape

  • Hooded: Blend shadow slightly above the natural crease so it shows when eyes are open.
  • Almond: Most looks work; emphasize outer V for depth.
  • Round: Focus on elongating with outer corner depth and soft wings.
  • Downturned: Lift outer corners with upward-blended shadow and liner.
  • Monolid: Gradient washes of color and shimmer across the lid look stunning.

Lip Shapes & Enhancing Tips

  • Thin Lips: Overline slightly with a lip liner close to your natural shade, gloss in the center.
  • Full Lips: Use deeper liners and matte or satin finishes for definition.
  • Uneven Lips: Balance by slightly overlining the thinner side.
  • Downturned Corners: Avoid dark shades only on the outer corners; highlight the center.

Color Theory & Undertones

Cool Undertones

  • Best in blue-based reds, pinks, plums, cool browns, silver jewelry.
  • Eyeshadow: taupe, mauve, cool greys, soft plum.

Warm Undertones

  • Best in corals, peaches, warm reds, gold jewelry.
  • Eyeshadow: bronze, copper, warm browns, olive.

Neutral Undertones

  • Can wear both cool and warm tones; neutral browns and rose shades are especially flattering.

Haircut & Styling Theory

By Face Shape

  • Round: Long layers, volume at the crown, avoid heavy width at the cheeks.
  • Square: Soft waves, side parts, curtain bangs to soften angles.
  • Heart: Chin-length layers, side-swept bangs to balance forehead and chin.
  • Long: Layers and waves to add width, avoid too much length without shape.

By Hair Texture

  • Straight: Blunt cuts, glass hair, or soft layers for movement.
  • Wavy: Long layers, shag cuts, and curtain bangs enhance natural texture.
  • Curly/Coily: Layered cuts, shape-focused cuts, and moisture-heavy routines.

Skincare Ingredient Dictionary

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Hydration, plumps the skin.
  • Niacinamide: Reduces redness, regulates oil, refines pores.
  • Vitamin C: Brightening, antioxidant, helps with dark spots.
  • Retinol: Anti-aging, texture, acne (use at night, slowly introduce).
  • AHA (Glycolic/Lactic): Exfoliates surface, brightens dull skin.
  • BHA (Salicylic): Unclogs pores, great for acne-prone skin.
  • Ceramides: Strengthen skin barrier, great for dryness and sensitivity.
  • Centella/ Cica: Soothing, calming for sensitive or irritated skin.

Nails & Aesthetic Vibes

  • Clean girl: Short, rounded, milky or sheer pink nails.
  • Soft girl: Pastel colors, tiny hearts, soft gradients.
  • Coquette: Bows, lace patterns, soft pinks and whites.
  • Classy: French tips, nudes, deep reds.
  • Glam: Chrome, glitter, rhinestones, long shapes.
  • Y2K: Bright colors, stickers, funky French tips.
  • Baddie: Long acrylics, sharp shapes, bold colors.

Fragrance Families & Mood

  • Floral: Romantic, soft, feminine (rose, jasmine, peony).
  • Fresh: Clean, energizing (citrus, aquatic, green notes).
  • Sweet/Gourmand: Cozy, playful (vanilla, caramel, tonka bean).
  • Woody: Grounded, sophisticated (sandalwood, cedar, vetiver).
  • Spicy/Oriental: Warm, sensual (amber, spices, incense).

Glow-Up Routine Ideas

  • Consistent skincare routine (AM + PM) for at least 4–6 weeks.
  • Hydration: water, herbal teas, hydrating foods.
  • Sleep: aim for 7–9 hours with a calming night routine.
  • Movement: light exercise or stretching to boost circulation.
  • Grooming: brows, nails, hair trims, lip care.
  • Style: build a small capsule wardrobe that fits your aesthetic.
