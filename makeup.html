Makeup Guide

Learn the basics of makeup, from everyday looks to glam, and discover techniques that flatter your unique features.

Makeup for Eye Shapes

  • Hooded Eyes: Focus shadow above the crease, lift the outer corner.
  • Round Eyes: Winged liner elongates the shape.
  • Monolid: Gradient shadows and smoked liner add depth.
  • Downturned: Upward wings create lift.

Blush Placement

  • High on cheekbones: Sculpted, lifted look.
  • Apples of cheeks: Youthful and soft.
  • Across nose: Sun‑kissed effect.

Contour Basics

  • Cheekbones: Under the bone for definition.
  • Jawline: Lightly along the edge for structure.
  • Nose: Soft lines for subtle shaping.

Beginner Makeup Kit

  • Tinted moisturizer or foundation
  • Concealer
  • Blush
  • Mascara
  • Brow gel
  • Lip gloss or lipstick
