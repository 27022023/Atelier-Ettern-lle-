Skincare Helper

Learn how to understand your skin type, build routines, and choose the right ingredients for glowing, healthy skin.

Skin Types

  • Normal: Balanced, minimal issues.
  • Dry: Tight, flaky, needs hydration.
  • Oily: Shiny, acne‑prone, needs oil control.
  • Combination: Oily T‑zone, dry cheeks.
  • Sensitive: Easily irritated, needs gentle care.

Basic Routine

  • Morning: Cleanser → Moisturizer → SPF
  • Night: Cleanser → Treatment (optional) → Moisturizer

Popular Ingredients

  • Niacinamide: Brightens, reduces redness.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Deep hydration.
  • Retinol: Anti‑aging, acne control.
  • AHA/BHA: Exfoliation and glow.
  • Ceramides: Strengthen skin barrier.
